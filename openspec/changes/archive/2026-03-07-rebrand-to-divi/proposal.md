# Proposal: Rebrand to DIVI

## Overview
Rebrand the application from "Casa Transparente" / "Financorp" to **DIVI**, adopting the slogan **"Divida sem Dívida"**. This change aligns the application's identity with its "FinTech Premium" aesthetic and high-performance core, while making the brand more memorable and engaging for all household members.

## Motivation
- **Memorability**: "DIVI" is short, catchy, and easy to turn into a household "verb" (e.g., "Lança no Divi").
- **Professionalism**: Adopts a modern "Startup" vibe that builds trust.
- **Value Alignment**: The slogan "Divida sem Dívida" explicitly communicates the application's purpose: transparent expense splitting without friction.
- **Consistency**: Finalizes the visual transition started with the "gooey" navigation and Inter font.

## Impact
- **UI/UX**: Update app title (title bar), headers, and the newly implemented splash screen with the "DIVI" logo and slogan.
- **Codebase**: Rename internal strings and references to the application name.
- **Documentation**: Update the memory-bank (`projectbrief.md`, `productContext.md`, etc.) to reflect the new brand identity.
- **Assets**: Implementation of the "D" icon/logo as established in discovery.
